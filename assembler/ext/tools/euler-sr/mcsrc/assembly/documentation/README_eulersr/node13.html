Quality filtering.

If you have quality values, you can trim low quality sequences: 

	${EUSRC}/assembly/${MACHTYPE}/qualityTrimmer -fasta reads.fasta	-qual reads.fasta.qual -outFasta reads.trimmed
The sensitivity of the trimmer may be tuned with -span S -minQual Q, where reads are trimmed so that the entire read is of min quality Q for each consecutive S nucleotides.
